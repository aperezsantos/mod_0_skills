Class: Lamp

Attributes:
type (string)
amount (integer)
size (integer)
lightOn (boolean)
lightBulb (boolean)
placement (array)

Methods:
breakLamp (changes amount attribute)
disconnectCord (changes lightOn attribute)
replaceBulb (changes lightBulb to false, then back to true)
moveArea (changes placement to add/delete area where lamp is placed)
